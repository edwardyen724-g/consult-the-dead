## Positioning Notes

**Why This Topic?**
Founders in acquisition discussions or considering exits are the highest-commercial-intent segment. The exit decision is not about their current product — it's about their next move. This is a decision that determines career trajectory and capital structure for the next decade.

**Why These Three Thinkers?**
- **Machiavelli**: The power broker — teaches optionality and leverage
- **Marcus Aurelius**: The doubter — teaches duty and honest self-assessment
- **Sun Tzu**: The strategist — teaches terrain and defensibility

**Tone**: This is not a cheerleading guide to exits. It's a framework for thinking through what actually matters — not just the money, but the power you're trading for it.
