## Email Personalization Strategy (Variant B)

Unlike Wave 1's "The Gift" approach (direct council recommendation), Wave 2 uses "The Reframe" approach:

**Subject line**: `Your [decision keyword] — what historical founders saw that you might miss`

**Hook**: Shared decision pattern from IH case study, pointing to their specific context

**Example**:
```
Hi [Name],

You've made an interesting decision recently: [specific decision from their case study].

Most guides would tell you to [common advice]. But I ran the same decision through a 
framework used by some of the most ruthless strategic thinkers in history — and they saw 
something different. 

The way they broke it down might save you 6 months of second-guessing.

[Debate link]

— Consult The Dead
```
